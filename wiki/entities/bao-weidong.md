# Bao, Weidong

Weidong Bao is a researcher at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Northeastern University (Shenyang, China). His research focuses on knowledge graph construction and large language models.

## Contributions

- **DIAL-KG** ([source](../sources/dial-kg.md)): Co-first author (equal contribution) of the DIAL-KG framework for incremental knowledge graph construction via dynamic schema induction and governance adjudication.
