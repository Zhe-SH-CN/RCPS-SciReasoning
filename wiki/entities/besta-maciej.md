# Besta, Maciej

Maciej Besta is a researcher at ETH Zurich, working on high-performance computing, graph algorithms, and AI systems. He is the corresponding author of the KGoT framework.

## Contributions

- **KGoT (Knowledge Graph of Thoughts)** ([source](../sources/kgot.md)): Proposed an innovative AI assistant architecture integrating LLM reasoning with dynamically constructed knowledge graphs, achieving 29% improvement on GAIA with 36× cost reduction.
