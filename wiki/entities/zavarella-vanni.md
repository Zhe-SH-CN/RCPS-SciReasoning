# Zavarella, Vanni

Vanni Zavarella is a PhD Candidate in Computer Science at the University of Cagliari, Italy. He received an MSc from the University of Lorraine, France, with a specialization in NLP. He previously served as a Scientific Officer at the European Commission's Joint Research Centre (JRC) for 14+ years, working on NLP, OSINT, and media monitoring. He is a former core developer of Europe Media Monitor (EMM).

## Contributions

- **Methods for KGC from Text** ([source](../sources/methods-kgc-text.md)): PhD thesis covering KG construction methods across digital transformation monitoring, AECO research mapping, and biomedical causal relation extraction.
- Co-author of 40+ research publications in international conferences and workshops.
